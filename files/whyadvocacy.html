Why this Advocacy is for me

(Directo, n.d.)

This advocacy is for me because I just think about what happens to babies with no financial support from their mother. Pregnancy must be a right, and we must have the right to terminate it if we're not ready yet. If we hadn't legalized abortion, many children would be suffering in poverty.

This is especially a necessity in the Philippines where abortion is illegal and many people live under poverty and will have a difficult time in pregnancy. Abortion must be legalized in the Philippines and be accessible to all to lessen the suffering of our fellow Filipinos.

This is why you should also consider fighting for abortion access, especially in the Philippines. There's nothing more cruel then letting a child be born without their basic needs such as food, water, shelter, etc.

How you can help

According to the Center for Reproductive Rights (2022), here are some things you can do to support this movement:

  1. Donate to abortion funds. Your donation will help making abortion access more accessible to more people. You can also support the Philippine Safe Abortion Advocacy Network (PINSAN), a non-governmental organization, in reaching the goal of a country where abortion is accessible to all.
  2. Speak up. Tell your friends and family about abortion access. Share the information on why abortion access is a necessity. To help others, we need you to speak up.
  3. Fight for the right. Show others you are committed to this movement. Convince lawmakers to fight for our side as well.
